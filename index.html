About Me

Hello! My name is Andrew Yen. Front-end web developer educated at UC Riverside Extension where I earned a Certificate in Full-Stack Web Development and developed skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript and responsive web design.

My huge passion for a wide variety of video games has given me some knowledge on UI design and an enjoyable user experience. My favorite genres of video games are platformers, fighters, action and tactical RPGS (role-playing games).

As a video game enthusiast who has seen all kinds of great and messy UIs, I aim to create clean, responsive projects that deliver a fulfilling, positive experience to the user.
