About Me

Hello there! My name is Andrew. I'm a Full Stack web developer educated at UC Riverside Extension where I earned a Certificate in Full-Stack Web Development and developed skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Express.js, MySQL, MongoDB and more!

My huge passion for a wide variety of video games has given me some knowledge on UI design and an enjoyable user experience.

My favorite genres of video games are platformers, fighters, action and tactical RPGS (role-playing games). All of these types of games have in common are satisfying and clear UIs.

As a video game enthusiast who has seen all sorts of UIs (the good and the terrible), I aim to create clean, responsive projects that deliver a fulfilling, positive experience to the user.

I'm excited to work with others to create great projects and learn new skills. Feel free to send me a message to chat about possible future projects here!
